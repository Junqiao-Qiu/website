About Me

I am Junqiao Qiu (you can just call me "Jun" or "JQ"), an Assistant Professor in the department of Computer Science at Michigan Tech. I received my Ph.D. in 2020 from CSE Department of University of California, Riverside, under the supervision of Prof. Zhijia Zhao. Before coming to UC Riverside, I completed my bachelor degree from Sun Yat-sen University. My research interests are broadly in the area of parallel computing, compiler techniques, and systems. My CV is here.

I'm looking for self-motivated students (RA/TA support) who are interested in doing system research. Feel free to contact me.
