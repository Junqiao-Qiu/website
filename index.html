About Me

I am Junqiao Qiu (you can just call me "Jim" or "JQ"), a Ph.D. Candidate in CSE Department of University of California, Riverside under the supervision of Prof. Zhijia Zhao. My research interests are broadly in the area of programming systems and runtime support for parallel computing. My CV is here.
